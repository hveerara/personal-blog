Cooking

Cooking brings people together My Mom and my Grandmothers are my source of inspiration when it comes to cooking. I often fondly recollect how artistic they were in bringing together the various ingredients and cooking fabulous and healthy food for family and friends. And they were very adept at bringing the family members together into the process of cooking. The kitchen used to be the most used place in the house.

Now, I continue the family tradition of enjoying cooking with my family :) and those are precious moments I rejoice.

Recently, I have been inspired to pick up on techniques summarized under the OPOS umbrella. This helps me save time and energy on some elaborate traditional recipes which used to consume a lot more time and energy.
